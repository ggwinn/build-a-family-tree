dollhouse instructions

When I was around 3 or 4 (yes, I have memories of that period), going to Build-A-Bear Workshop was always a treat. A cardboard box always accompanied this little treat. That cardboard box was special to me. I saw the peephole and punched out the door as the beginning of a home for my dolls, as I did not have a dollhouse at the time. I tried to plant the roots of my house multiple times, but each time, my mom would throw away the box. She just saw it as trash, something not to hold onto, something in the way of my toys or the living room. I saw a home . . . but this isn’t just about me; it’s about all of us.
